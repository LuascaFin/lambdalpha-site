I'm Luca Ascani. After over 25 years building and scaling internet companies, I invest — early and hands-on — in a small number of teams with the ambition and the market to become category leaders.

About

I started out in Rome at 20 in 2000, launching BuyCentral, Italy's first shopping comparison site (sold to Lycos), and ADVance, which became the country's leading digital agency (sold to NetBooster). In 2004 I co-founded GoAdv / Populis, one of Europe's largest digital publishing groups, listed on the Paris Alternext exchange in 2007.

I brought the TechCrunch conference to Italy and co-hosted Founders Forum in Rome. Today I invest as an angel and back funds as an LP, with a portfolio that includes Scalapay, Lendable, Pliant, Apron, BearNotes and Stacks.ai.
