I'm Luca Ascani. After almost twenty years building and scaling internet companies, I invest — early and hands-on — in a small number of teams with the ambition and the market to become category leaders.

About

I started out in Rome at twenty, launching BuyCentral, Italy's first shopping comparison site (sold to Lycos), and ADVance, which became the country's leading digital agency (sold to NetBooster). In 2004 I co-founded GoAdv / Populis, one of Europe's largest digital publishing groups, listed on the Paris Alternext exchange in 2007.

I brought the TechCrunch conference to Italy and co-hosted Founders Forum in Rome, and co-founded TAG DCU in Dublin, a startup campus inside the university. Today I invest as an angel and back funds as an LP, with a portfolio that includes Scalapay, Lendable, Emma, Bear and Gideon Brothers.
